Kò sì níí sí ẹnì kan nínú àwọn ahlul-kitāb (láyé nígbà tí ‘Īsā bá sọ̀kalẹ̀ padà láti ojú sánmọ̀) àfi kí ó gbà á gbọ́ ní òdodo ṣíwájú ikú rẹ̀. Ní Ọjọ́ Àjíǹde, ó sì máa jẹ́ ẹlẹ́rìí lórí wọn. àgbéga ipò gẹ́gẹ́ bí èyí t’ó jẹyọ nínú sūrah Al-’a‘rọ̄f; 7:176 àti sūrah Mọryam; 19:57) tàbí kí ó túmọ̀ sí àgbéga tara (ìyẹn gbígbé ara kúrò láti àyè ìsàlẹ̀ sí àyè òkè gẹ́gẹ́ bí èyí t’ó jẹyọ nínú sūrah Fātir; 35:10). Àmọ́ kò ní tàbí-ṣùgbọ́n nínú mọ́ pé nígbàkígbà tí harafi “إلى” bá ti tẹ̀lé “رفع” òkè sánmọ̀ keje ni Allahu wà nítorí pé inú sánmọ̀ yìí náà ni Ànábì wa Muhammad (sollalāhu 'alayhi wa sallam) gùn lọ láti lọ gba ìrun wákàtí márùn-ún wá lọ́dọ̀ Allāhu (subhānahu wa ta'ālā) lásìkò ìrìn òru àti ìgun-sánmọ̀ gẹ́gẹ́ bí Allāhu ṣe fi rinlẹ̀ ní ìbẹ̀rẹ̀ sūrah al-’Isrọ̄’. Kódà tòhun ti bí àìgbàgbọ́ Fir‘aon ṣe gbópọn tó ó kúkú gbà pé sánmọ̀ ni Allāhu (subhānahu wa ta'ālā) wà. Ó sì bẹ Hāmọ̄n ní ilé gíga fíofío kọ́. Ó fẹ́ yọjú wo Allāhu! (sūrah al-Ƙọsọs; 28:38). Nítorí náà
