Òjíṣẹ́ Allāhu (sollalāhu 'alayhi wa sallam) sọ pé “Dájúdájú ọjọ́ Jum‘ah wà nínú àwọn ọjọ́ yín t’ó lóore jùlọ; Wọ́n ṣẹ̀dá Ànábì Ādam ('alaehi-ssọlātu wa-ssalām) nínú rẹ̀ ní ìṣẹ̀lẹ̀ t’ó tayọ òye wa àmọ́ tí ó tẹnu Ànábì wa Muhammad olódodo (sollalāhu 'alayhi wa sallam) jáde tí ó sì darí yín pẹ̀lú ohun tí ń bẹ lọ́dọ̀ yín.” Ẹnì kan nínú àwọn t’ó gba hadīth yìí wá sọ pé “Kí ni ìtúmọ̀ “tí ó sì darí yín pẹ̀lú ohun tí ń bẹ lọ́dọ̀ yín”? Tàbí kí ni ìtúmọ̀ “tí ohun tí ó máa fi darí yín sì máa wá láti ọ̀dọ̀ yín?” Ibnu Abī Thanb sì fèsì pé
