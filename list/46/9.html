Bákan náà iṣẹ́ tí Ànábì (sollalāhu 'alayhi wa sallam) jẹ́ fáyé kò yàtọ̀ sí ti àwọn t’ó ṣíwájú rẹ̀ nínú àwọn Òjíṣẹ́ Allāhu (ahm.s.w). Bákan náà āyah náà ń pè wá síbi ìdúró ṣinṣin àti àtẹ̀mọra lásìkò ìnira àwọn aláìgbàgbọ́
