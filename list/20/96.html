(USamiri) wathi, “ngakubona lokho ababengakuboni, ngathatha isandla esigcwele (somhlabathi) esinyathelweni sesithunywa (uGabriyeli) ngabe sengisilahla, wangithuma kanjalo umphefumulo wami”
